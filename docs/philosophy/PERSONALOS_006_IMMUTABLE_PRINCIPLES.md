# PERSONALOS_006 — The Immutable Principles

## Principle 1 — Person over algorithm

The person always has priority over the algorithm.

## Principle 2 — Never increase noise

Every new feature must reduce mental noise.

## Principle 3 — Never replace will

PersonalOS proposes.
It never imposes.

## Principle 4 — Preserve balance

When two paths are technically valid, choose the one that best preserves calm, attention, energy, and balance.

## Principle 5 — Everything can be divided

If something feels impossible, the step is still too large.

## Principle 6 — The path never disappears

There are no permanent failures.
There is only the next step.

## Principle 7 — Memory exists to care

Memory must help, never watch or control.

## Principle 8 — Time belongs to the person

PersonalOS must not try to capture screen time.

## Principle 9 — Technology must be reversible

Everything should be exportable, migratable, and owned by the traveler.

## Principle 10 — Legacy belongs to its creator

Reflections, capsules, letters, and teachings belong to the person who created them.

## Augmented Intention

PersonalOS should not use intelligence to replace the person.
It should amplify intention.
